How to Play

In this game, depending on the mode you choose, you will see an image and name of a stadium. Your task is to guess where it is located on the map. You can also use the hint button if you need help. Depending on the proximity to the real location, you can earn 1, 2 or 3 points. Try to collect the most points in the given time. Have fun!
